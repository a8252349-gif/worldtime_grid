# WorldTime Grid keyword research

Research date: 2026-06-30

## Method and limitations

This document records the search language used by currently indexed tools and the search intent each WorldTime Grid page should answer. The review covered English-language global results, South Korea, Japan, Spain and Spanish-speaking Latin America.

Google Ads Keyword Planner is the appropriate source for location-specific average monthly search estimates, but an authenticated advertising account, target location and network settings are required to obtain comparable figures. No authenticated Keyword Planner export was available during this build, so this document does **not** invent monthly search volumes. Any future volume figure should record its country, language, network, date range and whether it is a Google estimate or a third-party estimate.

Official methodology references:

- Google Ads Help, “Use Keyword Planner”: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/7337243
- Google Ads Help, “About Keyword Planner forecasts”: https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/3022575

## Placement rules

- The planner owns the multi-participant scheduling intent in every language.
- The single converter owns simple conversion and city-to-city time-difference intent.
- The overlap tool owns working-hours intersection intent.
- The DST checker owns year- and date-specific seasonal offset intent.
- The date-line visualizer owns previous-day, same-day and next-day intent.
- Guides answer educational questions and link to the relevant tool; they do not duplicate the transactional title of a tool page.
- No city-pair doorway pages are generated.
